Original Prescription

"Take one tablet by mouth once daily. Take in the morning. May cause dizziness. Avoid potassium supplements. Monitor blood pressure regularly."

Important Safety Alert

This medication may cause severe dizziness, especially during the first few days. Avoid driving or operating machinery until you know how it affects you.

Storage Instructions

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep the bottle tightly closed when not in use.

If You Miss a Dose

If you forget to take your dose, take it as soon as you remember on the same day. If you don't remember until the next day, skip the missed dose and take your next dose at the regular time. Do not take double doses.

About Lisinopril

    ACE Inhibitor

How It Works

Lisinopril works by relaxing blood vessels so blood can flow more easily, which helps lower blood pressure and reduce strain on your heart. It blocks an enzyme that produces angiotensin II, a substance that narrows blood vessels.

Why It's Prescribed

This medication helps manage high blood pressure (hypertension), which reduces the risk of strokes, heart attacks, and kidney problems. It may also be used to treat heart failure or improve survival after a heart attack.
