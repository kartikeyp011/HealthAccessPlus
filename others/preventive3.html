Preventive Care Matters

Taking proactive steps today can lead to a healthier tomorrow. Discover how preventive care can help you maintain optimal health and prevent disease.

Benefits of Preventive Care

Early Detection

Identify health issues before they become serious through regular screenings and check-ups.

Cost Savings

Prevent expensive treatments and hospitalizations by addressing health concerns early.

Better Quality of Life

Maintain your health and vitality through preventive measures and healthy lifestyle choices.

Recommended Screenings by Age

Blood Pressure Screening

Every 3-5 years

Regular blood pressure checks to detect hypertension.

Cholesterol Screening

Every 4-6 years

Cholesterol tests to assess heart disease risk.

Diabetes Screening

Every 3 years

Blood glucose tests for those with risk factors.

Skin Cancer Screening

Annual

Regular skin examinations for suspicious moles or lesions.

Depression Screening

As needed

Mental health check-ups, especially during times of stress or major life changes.

Stay Protected with Vaccinations

Vaccines are a crucial part of preventive care, protecting you and your community from serious diseases. Our clinic offers a full range of vaccinations for all age groups.

  • Influenza (Flu) vaccine
  • Tdap (Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis)
  • HPV vaccine
  • Pneumococcal vaccines
  • COVID-19 vaccine and boosters

Healthy Lifestyle Tips

Balanced Nutrition

Eat a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats to fuel your body properly.

Regular Exercise

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week.

Quality Sleep

Prioritize 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night to support physical and mental health.

Stress Management

Practice meditation, deep breathing, or other relaxation techniques to manage stress effectively.

Don't wait for symptoms to appear. Schedule your preventive care appointment and take the first step toward a healthier future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Preventive care consists of healthcare services like check-ups, screenings, and counseling that are designed to prevent illnesses, disease, and other health problems, or to detect them at an early stage when treatment is likely to work best.

Most health insurance plans cover preventive care services without charging a copayment or coinsurance, even if you haven't met your deductible. Contact your insurance provider for specific details about your coverage.

The frequency of preventive screenings depends on factors like your age, gender, family history, and overall health status. We recommend scheduling an annual wellness visit where your provider can recommend appropriate screenings based on your individual needs.

During a preventive care visit, your healthcare provider will typically review your medical history, conduct a physical exam, perform or order recommended screenings, provide necessary immunizations, and discuss lifestyle factors that impact your health. They may also provide counseling on topics like nutrition, physical activity, and stress management.
